Visualizations

One of the most accessible (and fun) parts of data science is certainly the data visualization. As an avid fan of R, I enjoy seeing how far I can push ggplot2. Check out two of my visualizations: a sentiment analysis "heat calendar", so to speak, of Trump's tweets. The other is an implementation of the bar chart races that were popular on reddit not too long ago.
