Natural Language Processing Working Group

Caitlin Kennedy, Michael Bunting, and myself have been working through a udemy course on NLP in R as part of working group for Booz Allen, and as we have been meandering our way through the class, we have found the materials to be a bit... lacking at times.

From the start, we had kicked around the idea of creating some sort of product to share with the larger team once completed. While we certainly haven't closed the door on making a product, we have landed on making a repository of training materials that could be used by future enthusiasts to better understand R and NLP.
